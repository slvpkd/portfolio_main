About Sefton Downes

I'm Sefton Downes a Developer Thinker Maker Teacher Creator Drummer

Hi! My name is Sefton Downes.

I am an experienced and adaptable Senior Front-End Developer with a proven track record in delivering scalable, performant, and accessible web applications using TypeScript, React, and NextJS. With over six years of hands-on experience in software development — including roles at Papirfly, Jobs Done, and currently Insurwave — I have developed a deep technical proficiency and a strong understanding of agile delivery, component-driven architecture, and user-centred design.

At Insurwave, I’ve played a key role in modernising and maintaining core insurance technology platforms, collaborating across cross-functional teams to build reliable, high-quality products that meet complex business requirements. My approach combines technical leadership, code quality advocacy, and a passion for innovation — ensuring each project I deliver is efficient, maintainable, and aligned with business goals. With a solid foundation in software principles (OOP, SOLID), CI/CD pipelines, and modern tooling, I thrive in collaborative environments where I can mentor developers, contribute to architectural decisions, and push the boundaries of what’s possible in front-end engineering.
